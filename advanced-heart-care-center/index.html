Meet Dr. Sanjay Khatri

Dr. Sanjay Khatri is a highly qualified Pediatric Cardiologist with over 16 years of experience in diagnosing and treating heart conditions in children. His expertise in non-surgical interventions and compassionate approach to pediatric cardiac care has made him one of the most trusted specialists in Jaipur and Rajasthan.

Trained at premier medical institutions and with specialized qualifications in Pediatric Cardiology (FNB), Dr. Khatri is committed to providing the highest standard of care for children with heart conditions.
