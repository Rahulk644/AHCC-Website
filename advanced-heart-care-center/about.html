About Dr. Sanjay Khatri

Dedicated to providing exceptional pediatric cardiac care in Jaipur

Leading Pediatric Cardiologist in Jaipur

Dr. Sanjay Khatri is a highly respected Pediatric Cardiologist with a passion for treating children with heart conditions. With over 16 years of clinical experience, he has dedicated his career to diagnosing and treating complex pediatric cardiac issues with precision and compassion.

His special interest and expertise lie in non-surgical interventional procedures for congenital heart defects, allowing many children to avoid open-heart surgery while still receiving effective treatment for their conditions.

Dr. Khatri believes in a family-centered approach to care, ensuring that parents fully understand their child's condition and are involved in all treatment decisions. His friendly demeanor and ability to explain complex medical concepts in simple terms have earned him the trust of countless families across Rajasthan.

Our Approach to Care

At Advanced Heart Care Center, our approach to pediatric cardiac care is guided by several key principles:

Child-Centered Care

We understand that children are not small adults. Our diagnostic and treatment approaches are specially tailored to the unique needs of pediatric patients, with a focus on minimizing discomfort and creating a child-friendly environment.

Clear Communication

We believe parents should fully understand their child's condition and treatment options. Dr. Khatri takes time to explain diagnoses in easy-to-understand terms and encourages questions to ensure families are comfortable with care decisions.

Minimally Invasive Approach

Whenever medically appropriate, we prioritize non-surgical and minimally invasive interventions that can reduce recovery time, minimize discomfort, and allow children to return to their normal activities more quickly.

Evidence-Based Practice

Our treatments are based on the latest research and established best practices in pediatric cardiology. Dr. Khatri continually updates his knowledge and skills to provide the most current and effective care available.
